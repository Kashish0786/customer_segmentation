# 🚀 Data Analytics Project: Customer Segmentation

## 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to perform **Customer Segmentation Analysis** using **RFM Analysis and K-Means Clustering**. This helps businesses identify high-value customers, at-risk users, and opportunities to improve revenue through targeted strategies.

## 🎯 Objective

Customer segmentation is performed using a **behavior-based approach (RFM Model)**:

* **Recency (R):** How recently a customer made a purchase
* **Frequency (F):** How often they purchase
* **Monetary (M):** How much they spend

Additionally, **K-Means Clustering** is applied to group customers into meaningful segments based on their behavior.

## 🔍 What I Did

* Performed **data cleaning and preprocessing** to ensure data quality
* Conducted **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis** to evaluate customer behavior
* Built a **K-Means clustering model** to identify distinct customer segments
* Created **interactive Tableau dashboards** for business insights
* Explored **TF-IDF and Cosine Similarity** for additional analytical understanding

## 🧠 Customer Segments

Customers were divided into multiple segments for K-means such as:

* 💎 Platinum Customers
* 💙 Very Loyal Customers
* 🌱 Potential Customers
* ⚠️ Losing Customers
* 🚨 High Risk Customers
* 😢 Almost Lost Customers

These segments help businesses take **data-driven decisions** for marketing and retention.
